About The App

Device Info: CPU & System is a user-friendly application designed to provide comprehensive details about your device's CPU and system specifications. Whether you're a casual user or a tech enthusiast, our app offers easy access to vital information such as chipset details, device model, CPU architecture, memory type, and bandwidth. We prioritize user privacy, ensuring that only necessary data is collected for app functionality. With Device Info: CPU & System, you can effortlessly explore your device's capabilities and troubleshoot technical issues with confidence
