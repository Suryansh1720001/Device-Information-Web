Data Collection

Device Info: CPU & System does not gather confidential user data. The app solely collects information regarding the device model and manufacturer for reading purposes only; it is not stored anywhere. These data are utilized to identify the chipset, device name, CPU architecture, memory type, and bandwidth, among other specifications. Personal information such as email addresses, names, and addresses is not collected.

At Device Info, we uphold the principle of respecting your privacy. We do not gather or distribute any personal data. We firmly believe in your right to control your information and assure you that your personal information remains undisclosed and unshared.

Required Permissions

  • READ_PHONE_STATE - needed to get information such as the carrier names and the device IDs
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE - needed to determine if the device is connected to a network and what type of network it is connected to (e.g., WiFi or mobile data)
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE - needed to determine if the device is connected to a WiFi network and retrieve information about the WiFi connection
  • INTERNET - needed to access the internet to fetch device information and display it in the app
